What is Netsky Community?

Netsky Community is a Discord Community focused around technology.

Netsky Community was merged with Aurora Community on October 28th, 2017 and has been growing steadily to over 250 members.

Netsky Community is the hub for all things relating to the Crashie Brand, and AeroFusion. The Crashie Partners also reside there.

What is the Netsky Community ShareX uploader?

The Netsky Community ShareX uploader is a continuation of the aurorame.me ShareX uploader made by Jake#0009.

That sounds good. Can I join it?

You sure can! There are a a few things you need to do first. Firstly, you must be in Netsky Community. You can join from the link above. Secondly, you need to read the rules/restrictions. You can read them here. After that, you can apply for a key here.

I have joined. How do I set it up?

Download the ShareX template here. You can go to the ShareX generator also. Otherwise, open it in a text editor (such as Notepad++) and change INSERT_KEY_HERE to your key. Then just import it into ShareX.
